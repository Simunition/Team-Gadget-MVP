Tips:

Who - This should indicate the role of the person or persons because of whom the delinquincy exists, otherwise, leave as the unit being inspected. Ex: 10 of 12 contractors, gate guards, unit pilots, unit maintenance personnel, etc..

What - In a few words, describe the deficiency. Ex: Failed to complete training.

When - Single date or date range.

Where - Physical or non-physical location. Ex: Building XX, SCIF in building X, runway XX, Share Point/Share Drive.

Why - Root cause of deficiency. I.e. Lack of funding, insufficient manning, user error, etc..

Impact - Direct impact to mission or support functions, second and third order effects. Ex: Exposed unit to legal/financial liability, specific degradation to the mission, inability to maintain role proficiency, etc..

Additional - Anything which does not fit into the categories above (not required)
